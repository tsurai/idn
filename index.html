About

I'm a big fan of the protege effect or learning by teaching. Trying or even just pretending to teach others is a test of your own understanding and reveals knowledge gaps. This website is the still evolving result of my Indonesian studies. While I'm doing my best to research and verify the information presented here, I am neither a native, nor do I have the cultural background to pick up on all nuances. Corrections are always welcomed and very appreciated.
Feel free to use the email adress listed on my github profile or open an issue directly.
There are no accounts, no data being tracked or sold, everything is local to your browser.

Lessons

01 Basic sentence structure & context

The first introduction to Indonesian. Learn about SVO, the basic sentence structure, how to construct basic statements, as well as some interesting quirks that make Indonesian special.

02 Pronouns & Demonstratives

Learn to refer to yourself and others, the subtleties of formality and dive deeper into the essence of Indonesian and how to say more with less.

03 Greetings & Farewells

To have a conversation you first need to learn how to start one. Let's learn about basic some basic greetings, farewells and other phrases.

04 Conjugation: Tense & Voice

Strictly speaking, Indonesian does not have verb conjugation and uses modifications made with various affixes and adverbs instead. This chapter introduces these concepts and how to modify the tense of a verb or clause.

05 Asking Questions

The groundwork of communication is the ability to ask questions. For this we have to learn the Indonesian counterparts of the called wh queston words: what, who, why and so on.
